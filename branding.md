## Usage Guidelines

- **Primary**: Use #03fb80 for buttons, links, and key accents
- **Background**: Use #000000 for dark sections, #ffffff for light sections
- **Text**: Use #ffffff on dark backgrounds, #000000 on light backgrounds

## Logo Ideas

- Bold, modern wordmark
- Neon green accent element or icon
- High contrast black/white with green highlights
